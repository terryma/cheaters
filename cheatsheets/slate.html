Hyper key: ⇪ (⌃-⇧-⌥-⌘)

Window Management
Shortcut Description
⇪-H Resize to left half of screen
⇪-L Resize to right half of screen
⇪-J Focus to the window to the left
⇪-K Focus to the window to the right
⇪-R Relaunch slate
⇪-␣ Full screen
⇪-B Focus Google Chrome
⇪-T Focus iTerm
